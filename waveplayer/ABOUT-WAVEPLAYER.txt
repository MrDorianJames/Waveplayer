Waveform display with SoundCloud-style played/unplayed coloring and reflection effect
Real audio decoding via Symphonia with accurate byte-level seeking
Click and drag on the waveform to scrub through the track
Space bar to play/pause
Right-click on the waveform to open a file
Single instance enforcement via Unix socket IPC — sending a new file opens it in the existing window
Command line argument support with autoplay
Settings panel with volume, full-width toggle, and accent color options
KDE and COSMIC desktop accent color integration
Persistent settings saved to ~/.config/waveplayer/config.toml
High resolution waveform at 2048 buckets
